## Apps

### [PSID Studio](https://github.com/JF11579/psid-shelf-app) &nbsp;·&nbsp; [Live app →](https://psid-shelf-app.netlify.app)

Browse 50 years of U.S. household data in your browser. PSID Studio sits on top of PSID-SHELF and makes 6,585 variables across 34 topics searchable in plain English — no Stata, no R, no setup required.

![PSID Studio](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/JF11579/psid-shelf-app/main/docs/screenshot.png)

---

### [NYC Gallery App](https://github.com/JF11579/NYC-Gallery-App) &nbsp;·&nbsp; [Live app →](https://nyc-gallery-app.netlify.app)

Every art gallery in New York City, mapped. Each day the app surveys them all — any gallery with a new show turns its dot from gray to green, and stays green for eight days. A live pulse of what’s opening across the city.

![NYC Gallery App](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/JF11579/NYC-Gallery-App/main/docs/map.png)

---

### [Mt Washington Hillclimb](https://github.com/JF11579/mwarbh-hillclimb) &nbsp;·&nbsp; [Live app →](https://mwarbh-hillclimb.netlify.app)

Race hub for the Mount Washington Auto Road Bicycle Hillclimb — a 7.6-mile uphill race held annually in August. Race status, weather, course details, and registration in one place.

![Mt Washington Hillclimb](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/JF11579/mwarbh-hillclimb/main/docs/MtWash.png)

## Research & Analysis

### [Homeownership and Educational Attainment](https://github.com/JF11579/PSID_2026)

Analysis of parental homeownership effects on child educational attainment using three generations of PSID panel data. Finds a statistically significant gap of roughly one year of education between children raised in rented vs. owned homes.

![Family network graph](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/JF11579/PSID_2026/main/docs/network.png)

## Writing & Books

### [PSID for the Rest of Us](https://github.com/JF11579/PSID_For_the_Rest_of_US) &nbsp;·&nbsp; [Read →](https://joe-foley.gitbook.io/psid-book)

A plain-English guide to working with PSID data in Python — from downloading a raw extract to a clean, labeled CSV. Includes a synthetic dataset so you can run the full pipeline without a PSID account.
